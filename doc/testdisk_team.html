TestDisk is a powerful free data recovery software designed to help recover lost partitions and/or make non-booting disks bootable again. PhotoRec is file data recovery software designed to recover lost files including video, documents and archives from Hard Disks and CDRom and lost pictures from digital camera memory.

Many people have been helpful in making TestDisk & PhotoRec very useful (and free) programs! This page lists some of those who've helped us by the contributions they have made, or continue to make:

Main Contributors

Christophe Grenier

Location: Paris, France
URL: http://www.cgsecurity.org
He started the project in 1998 and is still the main developer. He is also responsible for the packaging of TestDisk & PhotoRec for DOS, Windows(tm), Linux (generic version) and 'Fedora Extras' distro.

Juan Carniglia

Location: Buenos Aires - Argentina
He is responsible for the maintaining of the Spanish version of the documentation.

Fiona Meg Riessler

Location: Germany
She moderates ComputerBase.de Forums where she helps German users. She is reponsible for the TestDisk & PhotoRec documentation in German.

Daniel B. Sedory

Location: USA
He has worked on the English documentation since 2001 and suggested numerous improvements. Some of his work can be found at: The Starman's Realm.
